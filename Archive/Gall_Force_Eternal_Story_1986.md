# Gall Force: Eternal Story (1986)

**Tags:** 🌍 Japan | 🎭 Pessimistic | 📚 Space-Opera, Military | 🧬 Rebellion, Evolution, Aliens

**Medium:** OVA (Original Video Animation)

**Creator/Author:** Katsuhito Akiyama (Director), Hideki Kakinuma (Writer), Kenichi Sonoda (Character Design)

## The Pitch

In the far future, an endless war has raged for centuries between two alien civilizations: the amorphous Paranoids and the all-female Solnoids. When a small Solnoid cruiser called the Star Leaf is separated from its fleet after a brutal battle, seven women aboard must defend a newly terraformed world called Chaos from the Paranoid fleet. What begins as a routine defense mission reveals a shocking secret that will determine the fate of both races—a secret hidden by the leaders of both sides.

## The Truth

The Solnoid ship Star Leaf, carrying only seven women (commander Eluza, second-in-command Rabby, pilot Lufy, officers Catty, Pony, and Patty, and ensign Rumy), becomes separated from the main fleet after defending a newly terraformed planet. The crew receives orders to rendezvous at planet Chaos to defend it from the Paranoids.

However, the Star Leaf crew discovers they are subjects of a secret experiment secretly orchestrated by the leaders of both the Solnoid and Paranoid races. The "Species Unification Plan" was designed to create a new hybrid life form by merging the genetic material of both warring species. The battle at Chaos was merely the catalyst for this plan to come to fruition.

The crew must not only defend Chaos from the incoming Paranoid fleet but also foil the plans of their own leaders, who have sacrificed the Star Leaf crew as part of their grand experiment. After intense combat, the surviving crew members manage to prevent the total destruction of both fleets but are left grappling with the revelation that their own leaders willing let them die for their experiments.

The story continues in subsequent installments (Gall Force 2: Destruction, Gall Force 3: Stardust War), where the survivors discover the true extent of the unification plan and its consequences for all of humanity's descendants.

## The Core

Gall Force is a deconstruction of war narratives that explores the cyclical nature of conflict and the dehumanizing effects of prolonged warfare. The series poses uncomfortable questions about sacrifice, leadership, and whether peace can ever be achieved when those in power benefit from perpetual conflict.

The all-female Solnoid society, who reproduce through cloning, represents a fascinating examination of identity and purpose—these women are literally designed for war, yet the series asks what happens when they question why they fight. The Paranoids, depicted as amorphous blob-like entities, challenge traditional sci-fi alien archetypes by presenting an enemy that is both utterly alien and surprisingly sympathetic.

The "Species Unification Plan" serves as a metaphor for forced assimilation and the moral costs of pursuing peace through radical means. Both races' leaders choose to sacrifice their own people to achieve their vision of unity, raising questions about whether ends justify means in the pursuit of peace.

Visually, Gall Force was groundbreaking for its time, featuring detailed mechanical designs and ambitious space battle sequences that pushed the limits of OVA animation quality in the mid-1980s.
