## Synopsis

A 1970 Czechoslovak science fiction comedy film directed by Oldřich Lipský. The plot revolves around time travel to the past to change the future. A group of characters travel back in time with the mission of assassinating Albert Einstein, believing this will prevent some future catastrophe. However, their attempts lead to humorous complications and unexpected consequences.

The film is notable for featuring what has been likened to a "selfie stick" several decades before its modern design was developed—making it a surprisingly prescient piece of speculative comedy.

## Why It's Obscure

This quirky Czech comedy has remained largely unknown outside of Eastern Europe despite its clever premise and inventive storytelling. It represents a unique blend of Czechoslovak humor and science fiction that rarely gets attention in Western archives.
