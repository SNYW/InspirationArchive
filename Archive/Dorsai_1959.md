## The Pitch (Non-Spoiler Breakdown)

In a future where humanity has spread across the stars, the Dorsai are a warrior race—genetically engineered mercenaries from the planet Dorsai, feared across the galaxy for their unmatched combat skills. When the Confederation of Suns faces imminent invasion by the aggressive Marius Empire, they turn to the one force that might save them: the Dorsai.

## The Truth (What It's Actually About)

At its core, Dorsai! is about what happens when warrior culture meets political complexity. Our protagonist, Anthony, is a Dorsai raised away from his homeworld who must navigate not just battles, but the intricate politics of interstellar conflict. The novel explores the tension between honor, duty, and survival—and asks whether a people bred for war can ever truly find peace.

## The Core (Why It Matters)

Dorsai! helped define the military SF genre and introduced concepts that would influence everything from Star Wars to Warhammer 40K. It's a cornerstone of the "Childe Cycle" and established Gordon R. Dickson as one of the genre's founding giants.
